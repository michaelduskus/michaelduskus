Michael is ...

Systematic, curious, and creative, Michael is a User Experience Designer with a background in visual communication. Located in San Francisco, California, he is currently the Lead UX + UI designer on an agile team at SocialChorus where he leads the design and strategy of mobile and desktop solutions for a fast-growing SaaS company.

A graduate of San Francisco State University, Michael holds a BA in Industrial Arts, and has been a guest speaker and critic at events held by the Department of Design + Industry. He is a member of the Interaction Design Association (IxDA), San Francisco, and his graphic design and branding work has been recognized by the Art Directors Club and AIGA SF. Michael leverages his formal training and experience in graphic design and brand development to deliver first-class user experiences that helps companies deliver compelling digital products.

Born and raised in a multi-cultural military family and prior military member himself, Michael has always been an explorer. He grew up in six different time zones and has experienced the world from various cultures and beliefs and draws upon these experiences to empathize with the user in his work. With his entrepreneurial mind, Michael brings an innovative spirit and enduring sense of humor to any team.
